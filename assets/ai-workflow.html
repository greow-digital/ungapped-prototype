The principle behind this workflow
Human creativity in. Human judgement out. AI handles repetition, variation, acceleration. Humans define direction, quality, taste. AI should not replace thinking — it should amplify it.
✓ What this workflow does well
  • • Removes 90% of mechanical work (transcription, structuring, drafting)
  • • Human review at the right step (editor stage 4 + design stage 5)
  • • Brand-voice context lives in Notion → Claude pulls it consistently
  • • One pillar case becomes 5–10 derivatives without re-work
✗ Anti-patterns I refuse to ship
  • • Publishing AI drafts without human editorial pass
  • • Generic "case study" template that ignores customer specificity
  • • Skipping the customer call to "save time" — the soul lives in the conversation
  • • Treating transcript as truth — AI hallucinates; editor catches
