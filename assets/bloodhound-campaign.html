🎯 Stand-out element · Campaign concept in detail

Bloodhound: cross-brand champion-tracking, powered by Profinder

When a buyer or champion changes jobs, they take their preferences with them. Our data sees them. Our outreach reaches them — across all three brands.

1
🛰️
Trigger detected
Profinder data flags a job-change among existing champions, buyers, or product users across the group.
Source: Profinder data + CRM cross-ref
Cadence: daily sync
Volume: ~80–120/mån est.
→
2
🧭
Cross-brand routing
Logic decides which brand the champion is most likely to bring with them — based on prior brand-fit + new employer's needs.
Input: brand-history, new-co ICP, role
Output: brand match + AE assignment
Engine: Claude + scoring rules
→
3
🎥
Personalized outreach
CSM/AE records a 60-sec Loom referencing prior wins. Email sequence delivered, sales-assisted, never spammy.
Channel: Loom + email + LinkedIn
Cadence: 3-touch over 14 days
AI assist: message-draft per champion
→
4
📈
Conversion + nurture
Booked demo or sales-assisted onboarding at champion's new employer. Wins logged in CRM with attribution to Bloodhound.
Hypothesis: 5–10× cold outbound conv.
Attribution: source = Bloodhound
Long-tail: nurture if not in-market
Cross-brand routing logic — examples
Ungapped champion → new role
If new employer is public-sector / member-org → Ungapped AE follows up. If new employer is enterprise/private → check Liana fit first.
Profinder champion → new role
Always default to Profinder follow-up (sales-data is universal). Add cross-sell layer if new employer fits Liana CMS or Ungapped use cases.
Liana champion → new role
Liana AE leads. If new employer is <200 emp Nordic → also flag Ungapped fit. If sales-org > 20 reps → also flag Profinder fit.

Why this is a group-CMO play, not a brand play
No single brand-CMO can build this loop. It requires Profinder data, three brands' CRMs, and one shared routing layer. When it works, every champion who changes jobs becomes a multiplier — and the Profinder brand grows on Liana and Ungapped traffic.
