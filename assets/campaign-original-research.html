Marketing Campaign · Stand-out Element
Original Research — The 2026 Nordic Sales Intelligence Benchmark
An 8-week original-research campaign anchored on Profinder, sourced from a survey of 200+ Nordic B2B sales & marketing leaders cross-referenced with Profinder's anonymized platform telemetry. Becomes a yearly compounding asset — 2026 · 2027 · 2028 — every edition stronger than the last as the dataset grows.
The angle that earns the report a second look
"In the age of signal-based selling, why are Nordic B2B teams still cold-calling 60% of their pipeline?"
Hypothesis: Despite intent signals, trigger-event data, and AI prospecting being commercially available since ~2020, the median Nordic SaaS sales team in 2026 still sources the majority of pipeline from cold outreach — because signal-based selling lacks playbooks, benchmarks, and proof points. This report fills that gap.
